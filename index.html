Privacy Policy

We put your game stats including how many games played and how many wins in your browser's local storage so you can share them with others and earn bragging rights.

IP tracking, Google Signals integration, USER ID generation, and ad personalization are not used. We only collect the bare minimum to come up with stats.

Technology and Inspiration

This site is inspired by Poeltl, the NBA player guessing game.

Darth Vordle was built in about a week using vanilla JS. The character database was created by gathering information from the Star Wars™ dictionary, Wookieepedia. The database includes revelant characters from the first 6 films to be relased, coming to a total of 82 characters.
